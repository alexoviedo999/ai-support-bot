Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 14-day trial work?

Start with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required. After 14 days, choose a plan that fits your needs or continue on our free Starter plan.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate any differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and undergo regular third-party security audits.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to learn more.
